ABOUT US

1E's Kimpinoy, established in 2024 by Colegio de Montalban entrepreneurship students, offers nutritious and delicious Filipino and korean rice meal dish combined in Rodriguez, Rizal. to make product unique flavour , Kimpinoy is a type of Pinoy dish and Korean dish Typically made with rice, seaweed, and various filling such as vegetables carrots, sesame seed, and pastil this is our Filipino way of improvising and giving a Filipino touch to a Popular rice meal. Kimpinoy are presented in transparent packaging containers that highlight the Kimpinoy. Our brand identification is strengthened and the whole product presentation is improved by the simple design.
